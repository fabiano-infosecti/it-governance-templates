## Purpose

This repository consolidates practical governance artifacts built and refined through real-world IT leadership experience. All documents are framework-agnostic templates — adapt them to your organization's context, size, and regulatory environment.

**Useful for:** CIOs, CTOs, CISOs, IT Managers, GRC professionals, DPOs, and security consultants.

## How to Use

1. **Browse** the folder relevant to your need
2. **Download** the template (Markdown or DOCX where available)
3. **Adapt** placeholders marked with `[BRACKETS]` to your organization
4. **Review** with your legal, compliance or information security team before rollout
5. **Version** your adapted documents in your own private repository

> ⚠️ These templates are starting points, not legal advice. Always validate compliance artifacts with qualified legal and regulatory professionals.
